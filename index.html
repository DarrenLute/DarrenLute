The Game District

The Game District is my current project. I am starting from scratch to create a site dedicated to managing campaigns for those running RPGs (Role Playing Games). I am currently in the process of creating design documents for the site.

The goal: Create an online space where people can relax and play rather than spending time managing the mechanics of the game. The software will do what it does best - manipulate the data and manage the rules - leaving the group to focus on the story.

The next step: Creating the Customer Journey Maps.

Crazy Hair Publishing

In the dynamic new world of publishing, authors seeking to enhance their presence often become their own publishers. By developing a unique publisher identity, authors gain credibility in the marketplace and can take advantage of a wider range of distribution options.

The goal: Create a site to serve as a platform for the author’s publishing company. The site needed to drive book sales as well as serving as a launch pad for future writing projects. The site was created using Wordpress because of the client’s desire to maintain the site herself.
