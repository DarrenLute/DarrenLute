DarrenLute.com

DarrenLute.com V2. This is the second version of this site. My goal was to clean it up a bit, get rid of the clutter and streamline the design. The site looks and feels much better than V1 already.

Still need to finish adding content, reduce the file size on the images, and then make sure that it works as well on mobile as it does the desktop. The site uses AngularJS, Sass and Flexbox.

Crazy Hair Publishing

It is a common practice among self published authors, to create their own publishing presence. It makes it easier to get ISBN numbers for their books and allows them to make use of a wider variety of distribution avenues.

The site needed to be a place that Su could sell her work and be a platform to launch future opportunities that her writing affords her. It also needed to be something familiar that she could easily upkeep herself. I chose a Wordpress site.
